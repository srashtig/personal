About Me

I'm a Data Scientist and Researcher with 5+ years of experience in machine learning, big data analysis, gravitational waves physics, and statistical modelling. I am working at the Max Planck Institute for Gravitational Physics (AEI Potsdam, Germany), enhancing gravitational wave detection and analysis tools to discover blackholes in the universe. With a Physics Ph.D., I bridge the theory of gravity with deep learning, Bayesian inference, and large-scale distributed computing. I am deeply passionate about AI, sustainability, and creating positive societal impact.
